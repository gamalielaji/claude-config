# Circadian Protocol for ADHD Founders

## The Science

ADHD is partially a circadian rhythm disorder (Luu & Fabiano, Frontiers in Psychiatry, 2025):
- 73–78% of ADHD adults have a delayed sleep-wake cycle
- Melatonin onset delayed ~90 minutes vs neurotypical
- Cortisol rhythms blunted and delayed
- Clock gene expression (BMAL1/PER2) attenuated

Stimulants can mask sleep deprivation — this is a time bomb (WashU, 2025).

## The Protocol

### Non-Negotiables (Do These First)

1. **Fixed wake time** — Same time every day including weekends. ±30 min max variance. This is the #1 circadian anchor.

2. **Morning bright light** — 10,000 lux light therapy lamp OR direct sunlight within 30 min of waking. Duration: 20–30 min. This advances melatonin onset.

3. **Evening light restriction** — Blue light blocking glasses 2–3 hours before target sleep time. Dim all room lighting. Screen elimination preferred; if impossible, use night shift modes + blue blockers.

4. **No caffeine after 15:00** — Half-life is 5–6 hours. Afternoon caffeine directly delays sleep onset.

### Optimization Layer

5. **Morning exercise** — Specifically advances circadian phase in late chronotypes. Even evening exercise helps night owls, but morning is optimal.

6. **Consistent meal times** — The gut has its own circadian clock. Regular meal timing reinforces the master clock.

7. **Low-dose melatonin as chronobiotic** — 0.5–3mg, taken 2–4 hours BEFORE desired sleep onset (not AT bedtime). This is a timing signal, not a sedative. One RCT: melatonin alone advanced DLMO by ~1.5 hours; melatonin + bright light by ~2 hours.

### Evidence for Multimodal Approach

A 3-week behavioral intervention (fixed wake time + morning light + evening restriction + no late caffeine/meals/naps) shifted DLMO, wake time, and peak cortisol by ~2 hours. Depression scores dropped 58%, stress 40%. Behavioral chronotherapy alone — no medication.

### Tracking

Log daily for 2 weeks:
- Wake time (actual)
- Light exposure (Y/N, duration)
- Caffeine timing
- Exercise timing
- Sleep onset time
- Subjective energy curve (rate 1–10 at 9am, 12pm, 3pm, 6pm, 9pm)
- Crash events (time, severity)

Patterns emerge within 7–10 days. Adjust the protocol based on individual chronotype.
