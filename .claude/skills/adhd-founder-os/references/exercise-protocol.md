# Exercise Protocol for ADHD Founders

## Why It Works

Exercise produces effects similar to stimulant medications:
- Raises dopamine & norepinephrine in prefrontal cortex (same targets as methylphenidate/amphetamine)
- Increases cortical volume in executive function regions
- Enhances functional connectivity between attention circuits
- Boosts BDNF (neural growth factor) supporting neuroplasticity

## Effect Sizes (From 2025–2026 Meta-Analyses)

### Acute (Single Session)
- Inhibitory control: Hedges' g = 0.55 (moderate, P < 0.001)
- Core ADHD symptoms: g = 0.23 (small, P = 0.024)
- Even ONE workout helps. Effect appears immediately post-exercise.

### Chronic (Regular Program)
- Inhibitory control: moderate-to-large improvements
- Working memory: moderate improvements
- Cognitive flexibility: moderate improvements
- Inattention: moderate reductions
- Hyperactivity/impulsivity: moderate-to-strong reductions

## The Prescription

### Minimum Effective Dose
- **Frequency:** 4–5 sessions/week
- **Duration:** 30–45 minutes/session
- **Intensity:** Moderate to high (>60% max HR; >80% for BDNF response)

### Type Matters
| Exercise Type | Primary Benefit |
|---------------|----------------|
| Aerobic (running, cycling, swimming) | Sustained attention |
| High-intensity intervals (HIIT) | Impulse control |
| Coordinative (sports, martial arts, dance) | Cognitive flexibility |
| Ball sports (basketball, futsal, tennis) | Inhibitory control + working memory (possibly BEST single modality) |

### Optimal Timing
- **Morning exercise** is ideal: advances circadian phase + provides dopamine boost at start of day when ADHD symptoms are often worst
- For late chronotypes (most ADHD adults), even evening exercise advances the circadian clock
- To maximize BDNF: >80% max HR, >40 minutes duration

## The Founder Integration

### Pre-Deep-Work Protocol
- 20 min moderate-intensity exercise before the day's hardest cognitive task
- Acts as "natural Adderall" — peak cognitive benefit occurs 20–60 min post-exercise

### Emergency Activation
- When stuck/can't start: 5–10 min high-intensity burst (jumping jacks, burpees, sprint)
- Not a full workout — just enough to cross the dopamine activation threshold

### Crash Recovery
- 10-min outdoor walk (light exposure + gentle movement)
- Replaces the instinct to reach for caffeine or phone

## Critical Advantage

Exercise is the ONLY intervention with:
- Zero drawdown (no side effects, no tolerance, no dependency)
- Compounding returns (neuroplastic benefits accumulate)
- Multi-system benefits (cardiovascular, metabolic, mood, sleep, cognition)
- Zero cost
