# Prompt Library — Copy-Paste Prompts for Every State × Phase

## Session Management Prompts

### Session Open
"Session: [project]. Pillar: [R1/R2/R3/R4]. Revenue thesis: [how this makes money]. Deliverable: [what ships]. Time: [X min]. Session [N] of [budget]. Redirect me if I drift — remind me of the revenue thesis."

### Session Close
"Session close. Log: 1) What shipped. 2) Revenue impact. 3) Next action. 4) Sessions remaining. 5) Handoff needed? Save to memory."

### Monday Triage
"Monday triage: What shipped last week? Active projects (max 3) — status + sessions left. Do I have an R1 project active? Top 3 this week with revenue thesis. Kill check: anything that should die?"

### Wednesday Drift Check
"Mid-week: Am I on Monday's plan? What drifted? What's at risk for Friday?"

### Friday Ship Review
"Friday review: What shipped (R1-R4 tags)? Revenue scoreboard. What didn't ship + why? Update active list. Pre-load Monday's #1 R1."

### Monthly Revenue Check
"Monthly: Revenue generated. Pipeline value. Mix (R1/R2/R3/R4 %). Shipped vs killed. Pattern analysis. Am I moving toward $1B or just busy?"

## State-Matched Prompts

### Can't Start (Dopamine Deficit)
"Give me the ugliest possible first draft of [X]. I don't want quality. I want something on screen in 2 minutes."

"What's the single smallest action on [project] I can do in under 2 minutes? Just the first atom."

### Drifting (Hyperfocus Misdirection)
"I've been deep in [tangent]. Park it — save where I am. Now: what's the actual deliverable I committed to?"

"How long have I been on this? What was the time box? What % of deliverable is done?"

### Overwhelmed (Executive Overload)
"Brain dump: [everything]. Revenue-rank each (R1/R2/R3/R4/not revenue). Top 3 by revenue impact only. Kill or queue the rest."

### Spiraling (Emotional Dysregulation)
"I'm in an RSD spiral about [X]. Separate the facts from the story my brain is telling. Then one action I can take right now."

### Crashing (Energy Crash)
"I'm crashing. Easiest, lowest-stakes task that still moves something forward? No deep thinking."

### Can't Decide (Decision Paralysis)
"Stuck between [A] and [B]. Is this reversible? If yes, pick one. Which generates revenue faster?"

### Deadline Panic (Time Blindness)
"Deadline: [X] in [Y hours]. Break into 30-min blocks. Minimum viable deliverable. Strip everything else."

## Project Phase Prompts

### Scope a New Project
"Scope this: [idea]. One deliverable, one audience, one deadline, one MVP definition. Which revenue pillar? Session budget?"

### Start Building
"Give me the roughest first draft of [deliverable]. Don't optimize. I need activation, not quality."

### Mid-Build Check
"What's done? What's left? Am I building the MVP or gold-plating?"

### Ready to Ship?
"Ship checklist: minimum remaining to send this. No nice-to-haves. Only blockers."

### Anti-Perfectionism
"This is revision 2. Done revising. Format, package, give me the final file."

### Tangent Parking
"Park this: [interesting tangent]. Save it. Don't let me work on it now. Back to [deliverable]."

## Team Handoff Prompts

### Build Any Brief
"Build handoff brief for [team/person] on [project]: revenue thesis, deliverable, constraints, locked decisions, references, review checkpoint, ship date. Make it so clear they execute without questions."

### Review Incoming Work
"[Paste work]. Review against original brief. What matches? What's off? Draft feedback — specific, actionable, max 5 points."

### Delegate Decision
"I'm doing [task]. Should this be me or should I hand it off? If handoff, which team and build the brief."
